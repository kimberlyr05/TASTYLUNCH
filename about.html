ABOUT THE APP


Welcome to Tasty Lunch

Welcome to Tasty Lunch a ticket organizer created to facilitate the weekly control of tickets for each student. We can choose how many tickets we will need for the week.

The positive change

The positive change of our App is to keep track of the lunch tickets used by each student, this also helps us save time when we wait for the delivery of lunch tickets and save material that is invested to print the registration slips at the time of picking up the lunch tickets.
